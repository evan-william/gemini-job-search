# Sample Job Posting: Junior AI Product Analyst

Company: BrightLoop Analytics

Location: Jakarta or remote Indonesia

Compensation: IDR 12,000,000 - 18,000,000 monthly

BrightLoop Analytics helps ecommerce teams understand customer feedback and
turn messy support tickets into product insights. We are hiring a Junior AI
Product Analyst to help build internal AI workflows and customer-facing insight
reports.

## Responsibilities

- Clean and analyze customer feedback data from CSV exports, survey tools, and
  support ticket systems.
- Build lightweight AI workflows using prompts, embeddings, and retrieval over
  product documentation.
- Create weekly insight reports for product managers and customer success.
- Work with engineers to test prompt quality and document failure cases.
- Communicate findings clearly to non-technical stakeholders.

## Requirements

- Python or JavaScript experience.
- SQL basics.
- Experience cleaning real-world messy data.
- Understanding of LLMs, prompt engineering, embeddings, or RAG.
- Strong written communication.
- Portfolio project, internship, freelance work, or coursework that proves you
  can ship small tools independently.

## Nice to Have

- Experience with ecommerce, customer support, or product analytics.
- Familiarity with dashboards.
- Basic evaluation methods for AI outputs.
- GitHub README writing and technical documentation.
